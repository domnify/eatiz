About Eatiz

The Eatiz restaurant was est in 2010 in Kozhikode, Kerala. With more than 10 years of experience and base on traditional recipes, we understand how to best serve our customers through tried service principles. Instead of following trends, we set them. We create our foods and meals, we’re proud to serve and deliver it fast, with a smile. No matter where you find us, we’re making sure each meal our clients enjoy is delicious and one-of-a-kind. Our dishes is always perfect and our professional team working hard to make you happy.

We pride ourselves on the quality of our food. We are constantly changing our menu to highlight what is in season and to celebrate the best local produce we can find. We try and cater for everyone with plenty of vegetarian, vegan, gluten free and dairy free options.
